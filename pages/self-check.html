Self-Check

Love yourself.

Physical

Take care of your body. It doesn't have to be anything intense or crazy. Make sure you enjoy it and try to follow a routine. Go on a run, ride a bike, do some yoga- or even just go take a walk.

Emotional

Become more in tune with your emotions by checking in with yourself. Feel them and work through them. Write in a journal, listen to a guided-meditation, or surround yourself with nature.

Spiritual

Whether you relate this to a religion or not, this is about taking care of and nourishing your soil. The main objective is to find a sense of inner-peace through finding your own purpose and meaning.

Intellectual

Take care of your mind. Whatever you decide to do, make it enjoyable and interesting for you. Do something that challenges it. Read a book, learn a new art, watch a documentary.

Social

Connection is important for each of us. Try reaching out to someone in your life. You don't need to talk about your rainy day. Catch up with an old friend, phone a family member, maybe even make plans with someone.

Sensory

Help bring yourself to the present by tuning into your senses- sight, smell, touch, sound. Burn your favorite candle, listen to relaxing music. Work on grounding yourself.
